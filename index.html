I am a Senior Scientist at NTT Research in the Cryptography & Information Security Lab. I am broadly interested in all aspects of computer science theory, with an emphasis on cryptography and quantum computing. Previously, I was an Assistant Professor at Princeton University, before going on leave to help launch NTT's new industry research lab in California and ultimately making the move permanent.

I completed a postdoc at {% include processname.html name = "MIT" %}, my PhD at {% include processname.html name = "Stanford University" %} and my bachelor's degree with Highest Honors at {% include processname.html name = "UC Berkeley" %}. I am a recipient of the NSF CAREER Award and the Sloan Fellowship, as well as three Best Paper Awards, given every year to the top paper at each of the flagship cryptography conferences.
